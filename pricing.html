Pricing Questions

Is there really a free plan?

Yes! Create a free account and start a 3-day trial - no credit card, no ads, ever. The free plan keeps a few starter lessons forever. Upgrade to Pro any time to unlock everything and the AI buddy.

What does the AI buddy do?

Byte is an always-available helper that speaks kid - friendly hints, simple explanations, and encouragement. It's included on Pro, Family, Teacher, School, and District plans.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. There are no contracts - cancel whenever you like and you keep access until the end of your billing period.

Is it safe for kids?

100%. KidVibers is ad-free, COPPA-minded, and kids under 13 need a parent's approval. We never sell data.
