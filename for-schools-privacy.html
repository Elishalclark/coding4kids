🏫 Privacy, Data & DPA for Schools

Everything a district, school, or library reviewer needs: what we collect, where it lives, how long we keep it, who processes it, and a signable agreement.

Last updated: July 2026 · Questions: support@kidvibers.com
The short version: KidVibers is ad-free and COPPA-minded. We collect the minimum needed to run the lessons, we never sell student data or use it for advertising, students' AI chats are never sent to outside AI companies, and a school can review, export, or delete any of its students' data at any time.

1. What data we collect

DataWhy
First name / display nameTo personalize the student's account
Username & password (hashed)To let the student sign in
Age rangeTo pick the right starting lessons & apply protections
Learning progress (lessons, quiz results, XP, tokens)To save progress and show teachers/parents
Parent/guardian email (family accounts)For consent and progress emails — optional for school-managed students

We do not collect home addresses, phone numbers, precise location, biometric data, or any special-category data. A content filter actively blocks students from typing personal info.

2. Consent (COPPA / FERPA)

  • Family accounts: a parent/guardian gives verifiable consent before a child can use the platform.
  • School/District accounts: the school provides consent for educational use on parents' behalf, consistent with FTC school-consent guidance. Students in a school group don't need an individual parent email.
  • KidVibers acts as a school official / service provider under FERPA — student data is used only to provide the educational service, under the school's direction.

3. Where data is stored & security

  • Hosted on Cloudflare (Workers + D1 database), served entirely over encrypted HTTPS.
  • Passwords are stored one-way hashed (PBKDF2) — we can't see them.
  • Sessions expire; staff actions are rate-limited; strict security headers (HSTS, anti-clickjacking) on every page.
  • The AI buddy ("Byte") runs on our own servers; student chats are never sent to third-party AI companies, never stored long-term, and never used to train models.

4. Sub-processors

We keep our vendor list short and privacy-respecting:

We do not use advertising or analytics trackers that profile children.

5. Data retention & deletion

  • A school admin can export any student's full record (JSON) or delete a student's account and all their data at any time from the dashboard.
  • Deleting a student removes their progress, quiz results, sessions, and messages.
  • Temporary "drop-in session" guest accounts auto-delete within 24 hours.
  • On request, we delete an entire organization's data within 30 days of account closure.

6. Your rights & controls

  • Review, export, or delete student data — self-serve from the District/Library dashboard.
  • Suspend or remove any student; reset logins; set school hours & screen limits.
  • Real-time safety alerts to the teacher, and a "report a concern" record for the KidVibers team.

7. Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

We're happy to sign a DPA for your district. It confirms KidVibers processes student data only under your instructions, as a school official under FERPA and in line with COPPA, with the security and deletion commitments above.

To request a signed DPA: email support@kidvibers.com with your district name and any specific terms your legal team requires, and we'll turn it around quickly. If your district has its own standard student-data DPA, send it over and we'll review and sign.
