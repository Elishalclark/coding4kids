v1.9 July 2026 Latest
🎟️ Library Sessions, Bilingual & More
🎟️
Live drop-in sessions
Perfect for library & classroom programs: a teacher taps "Start a session" for a code, kids tap "Join a Session" on the homepage, pick a nickname, and code instantly — no sign-up, no passwords. Change the code anytime; guest accounts clean themselves up afterward.
🔒
Session logout PIN & idle sign-out
On shared library computers, kids can end their own session with a PIN the teacher sets — and anyone left idle is auto-signed-out so the next kid isn't in their account.
🌐
Español — bilingual homepage
A one-tap English / Spanish toggle on the homepage (auto-selects Spanish for Spanish-language browsers). More of the app coming in Spanish over time.
💳
Monthly or Annual pricing
A billing toggle on the pricing page — pay month-to-month or once a year — that carries your choice all the way to checkout.
🔥
Streak calendar & daily bonus
A 14-day "coding days" calendar on your dashboard, plus a token bonus just for coming back each day.
🏅
Classmate leaderboard
Kids see how they rank among their own class this week — friendlier and safer than a global board.
📣
Class announcements & assignment tracking
Teachers post a message every student sees, assign lessons, and see exactly who's completed each one — plus a live "who's online now" view during a session.
🖨️
Printables: flyers & certificate packs
A print center with business cards, a promo flyer, and a session flyer (with a live-updating join code + QR), plus a printable class certificate pack for teachers.
🛡️
Safer & stronger under the hood
A content filter keeps profanity & personal info out of anything kids type, quiz answers are checked server-side (no peeking in DevTools), bulk roster actions, accessibility improvements, and automatic error monitoring.
