💬 Help & FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.

👦 For kids & parents
How do I reset a forgotten password?

On the login screen, tap Forgot password? and enter your username or email — parents & teachers get a reset link by email. Kids: ask a parent or teacher to reset it for you from their dashboard.

Does my child need their own email?

A parent's email is required (so we can keep you in the loop). A child's email is used during signup — enter the parent's email if the child doesn't have one.

How does parental consent work?

Every kid account needs a parent/guardian to approve it before the child can play. The parent gets an email link to approve, review, download, or delete the account anytime. See our Trust & Safety Center.

My child forgot their username.

A parent can see and change it in Settings → Your kids.

Is there a free plan?

Yes — start free with no credit card. Upgrade to Pro or Family anytime for the full curriculum and Byte. See Pricing.

🍎 For teachers, schools & libraries
How do I add my students?

On your dashboard, use the roster upload card to add everyone at once with a CSV (Name, Username, Password — one per row), or add students one at a time. You'll get a list of logins to hand out.

How do students join my class?

Share your class join code. Students log in, open their dashboard, and enter the code to join your group.

What is a "live drop-in session"?

Great for library programs. Tap Start a session to get a code; kids go to the homepage, tap Join a Session, pick a nickname, and code instantly — no sign-up. Guest accounts clean themselves up afterward.

Can students sign themselves out on a shared computer?

Yes, if you set a session logout PIN (dashboard). Kids can then end their own session with the PIN so the next kid can log in — a random kid can't. Idle sessions also sign out automatically.

How do I turn on two-step login (2FA)?

In Settings, toggle Two-step login (needs an email on file). After that, logging in also asks for a 6-digit code we email you.

How do I get a signed DPA / see your privacy details?

See Privacy, Data & DPA for Schools — it covers data, storage, sub-processors, retention, and how to request a signed DPA.

How do I verify a certificate is real?

Go to kidvibers.com/verify and enter the code printed on the certificate.

🛡️ Safety
What is the "Something's wrong?" button?

An always-visible button on every kid's dashboard. One tap alerts the child's parent/guardian, their teacher (in a class), and the KidVibers team right away.

Is there advertising or chat between kids?

No ads, ever, and no private messaging between users. Projects are private to each kid's own account. More in our Trust Center.

What happens if my kid types something concerning?

Every text field a kid can type into — the safety button, Vibe Studio project titles, and chats with Byte — is checked for concerning language (self-harm, abuse, bullying). If something's flagged, the responsible adult is alerted immediately by email, and the kid is shown a crisis-line resource (988, the US Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) right away — help doesn't wait on an adult reading an email first.

What happens to a session guest's account after a Live Session ends?

It's deleted automatically the next day — including anything they built — unless a parent chooses to save it right when the session ends (enter an email, set a password). See our Live Session privacy page for the full breakdown.

🤖 Is it real AI? Is it real coding?
Is Byte a real AI / chatbot like ChatGPT?

No — and we're upfront about that everywhere in the app. Byte is a simple, purpose-built helper for math, definitions, and coding hints — not a general AI model, and not connected to the internet. That's a deliberate safety choice: Byte's responses are predictable and can't say something unexpected or inappropriate to a kid.

Is this "real" coding, like Python or JavaScript?

It's "vibe coding" — kids build real, working websites, cards, and games by describing what they want, using templates they customize (text, colors, images) rather than typing syntax. It teaches the underlying concepts (variables, loops, logic) through structured lessons, while the creative Vibe Studio side is about making something they're proud of without syntax getting in the way.

💳 Pricing
Is KidVibers free?

There's a free tier with starter lessons and the avatar shop. Paid plans (monthly or annual) unlock the full lesson library and Byte. Libraries/schools running drop-in programs can use Live Sessions at no cost — see For Libraries & Schools.

Do I need a credit card to try it?

No — the free tier and Live Sessions (library/classroom drop-in) never require payment info.

👤 Accounts & data
Can I delete my kid's account and data?

Yes, any time — a parent/guardian can request full deletion of their kid's account and all associated data from their dashboard, and we'll confirm once it's done.

Do you sell or share kid data?

No, never. We don't run ads, sell data, or share it beyond the small set of infrastructure providers (hosting, email) needed to run the site — all under contract, none allowed to use it for their own purposes.
