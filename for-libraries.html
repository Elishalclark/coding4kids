How a session works

From setup to wrap-up in four simple steps.

1
▶️

Start it

The librarian starts a Live Session and gets a join code + printable QR flyer.

2
📱

Kids scan the QR code and pick a nickname. No account, no email, nothing to install.

3
🎨

They build

Kids make real web pages, cards, and fan pages with Byte. It feels like play.

4
📊

You get a recap

End the session and see how many kids joined and what they built.

Built for child safety & privacy

Everything your board will want to hear.

  • No ads and no selling of student data. Ever.
  • Guest sessions collect nothing personal — just a nickname, auto-deleted after 24 hours.
  • Content filtering blocks profanity and personal info kids might type.
  • Welfare alerts quietly flag worrying language to the adult in charge.
  • Session controls let the librarian lock the room and cap the number of kids.
  • A signable Data Privacy Agreement (DPA) is ready for your district.
