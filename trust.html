🛡️ Trust & Safety

Your child's safety comes first

KidVibers is built for kids - so safety, privacy, and security aren't afterthoughts. Here's exactly how we protect your child.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parental control

  • Every kid needs a parent or guardian to approve their account before they can do anything
  • Parents verify their identity to give consent (COPPA-minded)
  • Kids can't sign themselves out - only a parent or admin can
  • Parents can review, download, or delete their child's data anytime
🔒

Privacy

  • We collect as little as possible - just what's needed to learn
  • We never sell or rent your data
  • No behavioral advertising, ever
  • Optional "Sign in with Google" (grown-ups only) shares just name & email
  • Follows the spirit of COPPA (U.S. children's privacy law)
🚫

No ads, no creeps

  • 100% ad-free
  • No private messaging between users - none
  • No public gallery or sharing - every kid's projects are private to their own account
  • An always-visible "Something's wrong?" button lets a kid instantly alert a parent/guardian and our team
🛡️

Security

  • Everything served over encrypted HTTPS
  • Passwords stored one-way hashed - we can't even see them
  • Strict security headers on every page (HSTS, anti-clickjacking)
  • Logins and sign-ups are rate-limited to block abuse
🤖

The AI buddy

  • "Byte" runs entirely on our own servers
  • Kids' chats are never sent to outside AI companies
  • Never used for advertising or to train AI models
  • Conversation text isn't stored
🏫

For schools

  • Teachers and schools manage their own students
  • School-wide consent for classroom use
  • Suspend, edit, or remove any student account
  • Districts get custom branding and full control

💜 Our promise

KidVibers was made by a kid, for kids. We treat your child's safety the way we'd want our own treated - with no ads, no data selling, no shortcuts. If you ever have a concern, email support@kidvibers.com and a real person will help.
