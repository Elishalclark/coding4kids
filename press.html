📰 What is KidVibers?

KidVibers is a safe, ad-free, game-style platform that teaches kids ages 6-16 to code. Learners journey through themed worlds, beat boss battles, earn rewards and certificates, build real projects in a Python playground, share them in a moderated gallery, and get help from a friendly AI buddy named Byte - all in a kid-safe environment with verifiable parental consent (COPPA-minded).

💜 The story

KidVibers was built by a young creator who wanted coding to feel like an adventure instead of a textbook. It's genuinely made by a kid, for kids - designed to be welcoming, safe, and fun for every learner, not just the "naturally techy" ones. Schools, families, and homeschoolers can all use it.
